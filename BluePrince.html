BluePrince


​ Embark on a captivating journey through the enigmatic Mt. Holly manor in Blue Prince, a puzzle-adventure game that masterfully blends strategy, mystery, and roguelike elements. As Simon P. Jones, heir to the sprawling estate, your quest is to uncover the elusive 46th room hidden within its ever-changing halls. Navigate a labyrinth of shifting rooms, each offering unique challenges and secrets, in a mansion that reconfigures itself with every exploration.​
