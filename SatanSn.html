Satan Santa


Satan Santa is a short, first-person horror game that takes place on Christmas night, where a child's excitement for the holiday quickly turns to terror. As strange noises echo through a darkened house, the player must navigate the eerie environment, uncovering hidden secrets while trying to survive. The festive cheer of Christmas is twisted into something sinister as the player encounters a bloodthirsty, demonic version of Santa Claus.
