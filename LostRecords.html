Lost Records: Bloom & Rage


​ Lost Records: Bloom & Rage is a narrative-driven adventure game developed by Don't Nod Montréal and published by Don't Nod. The game is presented in two parts, "Tape 1: Bloom" and "Tape 2: Rage," offering players an immersive experience that blends 1990s nostalgia with supernatural intrigue.

Price: $39.99​
